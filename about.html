About Our Library

The Federal Polytechnic Ugep Library is a cornerstone of academic excellence, dedicated to supporting the educational and research needs of our students, staff, and faculty. Established to foster knowledge and innovation, our library houses an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources across various disciplines. Our mission is to provide a user-friendly, accessible, and modern library system that empowers users to explore, learn, and grow. With features like online catalog searching, book reservations, and user management, we strive to create a seamless experience for all members of our community.
